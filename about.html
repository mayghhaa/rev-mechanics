About Us

We are Rev Mechanics, a dynamic team of students from VIT Vellore with a passion for engineering and innovation in the field of motorsports. Our journey began with the aim of applying engineering fundamentals to create a world-class MotoGP-style racing bike and participating in prestigious international competitions such as MotoStudent Spain. We believe that true learning happens beyond the classroom, and through collaboration, creativity, and relentless hard work, we strive to build revolutionary vehicles that showcase our technical prowess and dedication.

Team Roles

Design Engineers

Focused on designing various components of the motorcycles using Autodesk's Fusion 360. They iterate designs for the swingarm, chassis, triple fork, and other critical parts, ensuring that every component is optimized for performance and manufacturability.

Simulation and Testing Team

Responsible for performing stress tests, heat analysis, and vibration impact tests on the designs using simulation tools to ensure they meet performance standards under racing conditions.

Powertrain Specialists

Focus on developing and testing the powertrain, including engine and rear wheel sprockets. Their expertise is crucial in balancing acceleration, torque, and top speed for our bikes.

Manufacturing and Assembly Team

Work on translating designs into physical products, addressing challenges in manufacturing complex components such as carbon fiber parts and ensuring that each part is built to exact specifications.

Aerodynamics and Vehicle Dynamics Engineers

Responsible for optimizing the aerodynamics of the bike to reduce drag and improve performance on the track.

Unique Ideas

Generative Design Approach

One of our standout innovations is the use of generative design for critical motorcycle components like the swingarm and chassis. By applying forces in simulations and iterating designs, we've created components that are both lightweight and strong, pushing the boundaries of what is possible in terms of aesthetics and performance (GENERATIVE MANUFACTURING).

Collaboration with Autodesk

Our collaboration with Autodesk has allowed us to utilize advanced tools in Fusion 360, enabling us to perform sophisticated design simulations and testing. The cloud-based collaboration has streamlined our teamwork, helping us iterate designs faster and more efficiently.

Reviving Scrap Vehicles

By converting scrap vehicles into electric ones, we've combined sustainability with innovation. This project showcases our ability to tackle real-world challenges while also promoting environmentally friendly practices.
