About Rev Mechanics

Rev Mechanics is the only Moto student team at VIT, dedicated to applying engineering fundamentals in designing and building a world-class Moto-GP style racing bike. Their vision is to foster a culture of constant innovation and improve continuously on the racing track. The ultimate goal is to develop a revolutionary internal combustion superbike for Moto-GP 2025.
